COURSE CONTENTS

01

Trading Landscape Overview

Understand the complete eTrading ecosystem from market structure to regulatory frameworks.

  • Market microstructure, Flows, Algorithms
  • Systems study: Exchanges, Dark pools, MTFs, ECNs, Market Makers, SIs
  • Regulatory landscape (MiFID II, Dodd-Frank, etc.)
02

System Components

Explore the technical architecture of modern trading systems and their interconnections.

  • Marketdata, OMS, Connectivity, Smart Order Router, Strategies
  • Performance: Architectural KPIs, SLAs, Latency Game
  • Trading infrastructure
03

Risk & Compliance Integration

Learn how risk management and compliance are woven into every aspect of trading systems.

  • Concept of Risk, pre/post trade risk checks

  • Regulations: motivations, impact and implementation
04

Real-World Application

Examples and activities to apply knowledge in real-world scenarios.

  • Case studies: Tech issues, Control failures, Flash Crashes
  • Understading Repercussions of poor design choices
  • Cross-team collaboration exercises
